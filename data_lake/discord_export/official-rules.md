# AutoScientist Challenge — official rules (extracted from Discord)

Source: pinned message by **Ross @ Adaption** in the #autoscientist-challenge channel,
2026-06-16, reproduced from the channel export. Only official Adaption-staff content is
reproduced here; participant messages are not (the raw export is gitignored).

> **AutoScientist Challenge Rules:**
> - Submit to one of the 10 categories across Part 1, Part 2 or both.
> - Release your adapted dataset to Hugging Face and Kaggle
> - Release your model weights to Hugging Face and Kaggle
> - Show measurable improvement against the baseline on our held-out test sets
> - Bonus: post on LinkedIn or X tagging @adaption_ai / adaption-labs with a demo
>
